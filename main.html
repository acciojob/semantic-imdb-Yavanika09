Summary

"The Shawshank Redemption" is a story of hope, friendship, and perseverance. It follows Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongfully convicted of murder, as he navigates life in Shawshank prison. Over the years, he builds a remarkable friendship with Red, a fellow inmate, and discovers a way to survive and ultimately triumph over adversity.
